1. Purpose

This document lists the information Jones & Associates requires from Heritage Community Bank to perform the Threat-Informed Cyber Risk Assessment defined in the Engagement Charter (HCB-EC-001).

This engagement responds to three FDIC Matters Requiring Attention (MRA 2026-IT-001, MRA 2026-IT-002, MRA 2026-IT-003) issued March 28, 2026. The request list is structured so that governance and risk appetite items (Waves 1–3) support the 90-day regulatory deadline, while the full evidence base (Waves 4–7) supports the 120-day independent assessment deadline.

Each item will be reviewed during the kickoff meeting, assigned a Bank point of contact, and tracked through its lifecycle. No assessment work will begin on any category until the items in that category have been received and verified by the Firm.

2. Submission Instructions

  • All items should be uploaded to the secure data environment (Heritage_Risk_Assessment_Data VPC) via the Bank upload portal
  • Accepted formats: PDF, Excel, Word, CSV, or screenshot
  • Each upload should reference the Request ID (e.g., IR-001) in the filename
  • Target response time: 5 business days per item unless otherwise agreed during the kickoff meeting
  • Questions about any request should be directed to the Engagement Lead
